### Ministerial meetings

The [UK Government](https://www.gov.uk/government/government-efficiency-transparency-and-accountability), [Scottish Government, and Scottish Parliament](https://www.lobbying.scot/SPS/LobbyingRegister/SearchLobbyingRegister) publish data on meetings between lobbyists and government officials. This consists of officials names, dates, the names of the organisations they met and the purpose of the meeting.

Transparency International UK has compiled this data on the [Open Access UK](https://openaccess.transparency.org.uk/) platform. Here's a [presentation about it](https://tictec.mysociety.org/event/using-civic-tech-to-supercharge-lobbying-transparency/).
